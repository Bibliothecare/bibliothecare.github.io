About

Fotografia is a leading American gallery with a focus on contemporary photography and multimedia art. The gallery, established in 1991, is housed in a converted warehouse with a large exhibition and printroom space New York City, New Yorks main gallery precinct. Fotografia represents both emerging and established artists, and has a long history of fostering artists who work at the forefront of contemporary photo media practice. work.
